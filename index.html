ABOUT US

SB Gemmological Solutions is a specialist firm with extensive local and international experience. We work hand in hand to execute every facet of the gemmological value chain, providing knowledge, consultation and bespoke services.

With over 11 years of experience in education and gemmology, we are committed to delivering excellence and quality in everything we do.

WHAT WE OFFER

QUALITY CONTROL SOLUTIONS

+

GEMMOLOGICAL LABORATORY

Setting up a gemmological laboratory to international standards as per ISO/IEC 17025 and ILAC accreditations. We cover facility layout, specialized equipment, personnel, training, operations, infrastructure, and adherence to standards, all of which are customisable depending on your requirements.

QUALITY CONTROL DEPARTMENT

Setting up a QC department is a foundational step for consistent product standards, reducing issues, and meeting expectations. Our services are customisable for manufacturing, wholesale, or retail setups.

QUALITY ASSURANCE TRAINING

We train staff in quality control procedures, gemmology, equipment use, and audit services, adhering to appropriate compliances and standards.

PURCHASE OF ADVANCED GEMMOLOGICAL EQUIPMENT

For retail setup, we advise all things related to purchase of gemmological equipment, whether advanced or basic, including tools like microscopes, UV machines, FTIR, Raman, and PL Spectroscope.
